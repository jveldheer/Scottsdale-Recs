Scottsdale Eats & Drinks

A curated guide from your boy Jared. Follow this and you'll eat well the entire trip.

Admission price: 6-pack of LGO English Muffins
🍳

Breakfast

Breakfast
Matt's Big Breakfast
Camelback & 24th St, Phoenix
This is the location closest to where you're staying. Classic, no-frills, phenomenal breakfast. Get there on the early side.
Breakfast / Smoothies
D-Lite
Old Town Scottsdale
Order the Cali Club breakfast sandwich and a Breakfast Buzz smoothie. Out of this world, super good.
Breakfast / Lunch / Shopping
La Grande Orange
40th St & Campbell, Phoenix
Phenomenal breakfast and a great lunch option too. Their sourdough English muffins are out of this world. They have a little market attached with candles and souvenirs that the girls will love. Seriously, don't skip this place.
🥗

Lunch

Burgers
Ingo's Tasty Food
Directly across from La Grande Orange
Really well-done burgers. Their lacto-fermented hot sauce is fantastic, and their homemade shattered chips are the move. Nice outdoor eating area, cool concept.
Healthy
Flower Child
Camelback & 44th St, Phoenix
Healthier food that's actually really good and really flavorful. Close to the Camelback trailheads if you pair it with a hike.
Lunch
Original ChopShop
Old Town Scottsdale
Great lunch spot right in Old Town. Solid option if you're out exploring that area.
🥩

Dinner

Steak
Steak 44
44th St & Camelback, Phoenix
Top-tier steak dinner. If you're doing one big steak night, this is it.
Steak
Mastro's City Hall
Old Town Scottsdale
Another excellent steak option. You really can't go wrong with either Steak 44 or Mastro's.
Fine Dining
Cafe Monarch
Old Town Scottsdale
Multi-course fine dining experience. You'll need a reservation. More of a date night spot, but the food is really, really good. At least put it on your radar.
Fine Dining
Elements
Sanctuary on Camelback, Paradise Valley
Great fine dining with incredible views. If you go, tell Chef Beau that Jared says hi.
Italian
Cibo
Downtown Phoenix (about 20 min from Scottsdale)
Fantastic Italian food in a really cool old-world venue. Worth the drive into Phoenix. I would be remiss if I didn't put this on your list.
New American
The Henry
Camelback Rd, Phoenix
Casual dining with a nice atmosphere. Really well-done, composed dishes. New American style.
Pizza / Chicken
Dough Bird
Phoenix
Good pizzas and chicken. Solid, easy dinner option.
Pizza
Grimaldi's
Old Town Scottsdale
Classic, easy pizza in Old Town. Great if you're looking for something simple and reliable.
🍺

Coffee & Beer

Coffee
Cartel Coffee Lab
Old Town Scottsdale
Probably the best specialty coffee in the Scottsdale area.
Brewery / Food
Arizona Wilderness Brewing Company
Gilbert (not far from Old Town)
Fantastic beer and a legit scratch kitchen with great gastropub food. I've brewed a couple beers with these guys. If you stop in, ask if Jonathan or Patrick are around and tell them Jared says hi.
Brewery
Wren House Brewing
Phoenix
Really good beer. Multiple GABF medals. No food, but the beer speaks for itself.
🥾

Hikes

Hike
Echo Canyon Trail (Camelback Mountain)
Near Camelback & 44th St
Great hike. Shouldn't take more than 90 minutes. I'd be doing this if I were in town.
Hike
Piestewa Peak
Phoenix Mountains Preserve
Another great hike, also under 90 minutes. Both of these are must-dos.
